Mouse To Click Buttons
WASD or Mouse to move cursor

Objective: Defeat all the enemy Units!

Unit Info:
Movement: How far the unit can move.
Range: How far a unit can perform an attack.
Damage: How much damage a unit's attack will do.

Attack Types:
Point: Attack will be performed on the selected point on the map.
Line: Attack will be performed on all the enemys in a specified direction leading up to the cursor.
Spread: Attack will effect an area around the specified point on the map.
